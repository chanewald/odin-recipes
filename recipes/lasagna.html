Lasagna

This is a recipe for a very delicious lasagna. Since my family has a dairy allergy, we will be doing with without dairy products like cheese.

To make this recipe, you will need the following ingredients:

  • 1 pound lasagna noodle
  • 2 jars of your favorite pasta sauce. I like raos
  • 3 packages of violife mozzarella flavored shreds
  • 1 pound mild italian sausage
  • Italian herb blend
  • Garlic powder
  • Follow your heart brand parmesan shreds

put them together and make magic happen
